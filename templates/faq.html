Getting Started

New to PvPocket? Start here to learn the basics.

PvPocket is a comprehensive companion app for Pokemon Trading Card Game players. It helps you:

  • Build and test decks with our 20-card format builder
  • Track your collection of Pokemon cards with detailed inventory management
  • Simulate battles against AI opponents to refine your strategies
  • Analyze the meta-game with real-time tournament data and trends
  • Connect with friends to share decks and collaborate on strategies

Whether you're a casual player or a competitive tournament participant, PvPocket provides tools to improve your game.

Creating an account is simple and secure:

  1. Click the "Login" button in the top right corner
  2. Choose "Sign in with Google" (we use Google OAuth for security)
  3. Authorize PvPocket to access your basic Google account info
  4. Choose a unique username for your profile
  5. Start building decks and tracking your collection!
Why Google OAuth? We use Google's authentication system so you don't need to create another password to remember. Your Google credentials are never stored on our servers.

Deck Building

Master the art of Pokemon TCG deck construction.

PvPocket uses a streamlined 20-card format designed specifically for digital testing and quick battles:

  • 20 cards total: More focused than traditional 60-card decks
  • 2-copy limit: Maximum of 2 copies of any single card (by name)
  • Basic Pokemon required: Must include at least 3 Basic Pokemon
  • Energy optimization: Automatic energy curve analysis

This format maintains Pokemon TCG strategy depth while enabling faster testing and iteration. It's perfect for developing core deck concepts that can later be expanded to full 60-card tournament decks.

Building your first deck is easy with our guided process:

  1. Go to Deck Builder: Click "Build Deck" from the main menu
  2. Choose a strategy: Start with a Basic Pokemon you want to focus on
  3. Add Pokemon: Include 3-6 Basic Pokemon and their evolutions
  4. Add Trainer cards: Include draw support, search cards, and utility trainers
  5. Add Energy: Usually 6-8 energy cards matching your Pokemon's types
  6. Test and refine: Use the battle simulator to test your deck

Our deck builder shows real-time validation and suggestions to help you create a balanced deck.

A strong Pokemon TCG deck typically includes:

Pokemon (8-12 cards):
  • 3-4 Basic Pokemon as attackers
  • Evolution chains for your main strategy
  • Support Pokemon with useful abilities
Trainer Cards (6-10 cards):
  • Draw support (Professor's Research, etc.)
  • Search cards (Ultra Ball, etc.)
  • Utility trainers for your strategy

Energy (6-8 cards): Match your Pokemon's energy requirements, usually focusing on 1-2 types.

Pro Tip: Use our deck analyzer to check your energy curve and ensure you have the right balance of Pokemon, Trainers, and Energy.

Technical Support

Troubleshooting and technical assistance.

Performance issues can usually be resolved with these steps:

  1. Clear browser cache: Refresh cached files (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
  2. Check internet connection: Ensure you have a stable connection
  3. Update your browser: Use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
  4. Disable browser extensions: Ad blockers or extensions might interfere
  5. Try incognito/private mode: Test if the issue persists in private browsing

If problems continue, please contact our support team with details about your browser and operating system.

  • Mobile browsers: Works on iOS Safari, Android Chrome, and other mobile browsers
  • Touch interface: Optimized touch controls for deck building and battles
  • Responsive design: Adapts to different screen sizes and orientations
  • Offline features: Some functionality works without an internet connection

For the best mobile experience, we recommend adding PvPocket to your home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA).

Account & Privacy

Your account security and privacy questions.

PvPocket collects only the information necessary to provide our Pokemon TCG services:

  • Google account info: Name, email, and profile picture (via OAuth)
  • Game data: Your decks, collection, battle history, and preferences
  • Usage analytics: Anonymized data about feature usage and performance
  • Technical data: Browser type, device info for optimization

We never sell your data, and we use industry-standard encryption to protect your information. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Updating your profile is easy:

  1. Go to Profile: Click your username in the top right corner
  2. Edit information: Update your username, bio, or preferences
  3. Choose profile icon: Select from our collection of Pokemon-themed icons
  4. Privacy settings: Control who can see your decks and collection
  5. Save changes: Your updates are saved automatically

Your Google account information (name, email) comes from Google and can only be changed in your Google account settings.

You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time:

  1. Export your data: Download your decks and collection data first (optional)
  2. Go to Profile settings: Find the account deletion option
  3. Confirm deletion: Follow the confirmation process
  4. Data removal: All your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to export any data you want to keep before deleting your account.

Limited functionality is available without an account:

  • Browse cards: View the complete Pokemon TCG database
  • View public decks: See decks shared by other users
  • Read meta analysis: Access competitive statistics and trends
  • Use tools: Access calculators and reference materials

However, to build decks, track your collection, save battles, and use social features, you'll need to create a free account with Google OAuth.
